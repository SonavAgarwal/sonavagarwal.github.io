About Me

Hi! I'm Sonav Agarwal, a student at UCLA studying computer science. My resume is here.

I love learning, coding, singing, swimming, and helping others. I frequently apply my interests in these areas to various projects I work on. It is a passion of mine to use my computer science and design skills to help others!

Activities

Currently, I'm working as a developer for Nova for Good, a club building tech solutions for nonprofits.

I'm also at LA Hacks, working on organizing and planning the event.

In high school, I competed in Speech and Debate at MVLASD where I qualified to the Tournament of Champions in Public Forum, and contributed as the PR/Social chair. I was also the lead organizer for MVHacks 5.0. I was the varsity swim captain and played varsity water polo for four years.

My Story

I started coding in Scratch when I was eight, and have loved it ever since. Now, I've taken CS courses, led CS clubs, participated in hackathons, and built real-world solutions.

I am also a graphic design enthusiast. Whether it's mocking up an app, creating a flyer, or making a Minecraft skin, I love turning ideas into designs. I use my artistic abilities everywhere from schoolwork to marketing my projects.

In my free time, I love karaokeing or playing games with my friends.
